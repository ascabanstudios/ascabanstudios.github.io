1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, phone number, medical information, and any other data provided during registration or use of the app.
  • Payment Information: We collect payment details to process transactions for services like doctor consultations and medical records.

b. Permissions

  • Camera & Microphone: For video consultations and capturing images for medical purposes.
  • Storage: To save medical documents, prescriptions, and images.
  • Network & Wi-Fi Access: To connect to the internet for consultations and services.
  • SMS: For OTP verification and appointment reminders.
  • External Storage: To store and access files related to your health records and documents.
  • Phone State: To detect when a call is made and adjust accordingly during consultations.

c. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, IP address, and unique device identifiers.
  • App Usage: Interaction with the app, session duration, and features used.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: Managing consultations, appointments, and medical records.
  • Payment Processing: Handling payments through third-party services like Razorpay.
  • Improving the App: Enhancing features and user experience based on how you use the app.
  • Sending Notifications: For appointment reminders, updates, and other relevant communications.
  • Compliance and Legal: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information under the following circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: To provide medical consultations and manage patient data.
  • Payment Processors: Payment information is processed by third parties like Razorpay for secure transactions.
  • Third-Party Services: For notifications via OneSignal and Firebase.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may share your data to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. You can request the deletion of your data by contacting us directly.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. While we take all reasonable precautions, no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security.
